# Product Strategy

## Positioning
Performance OS is a **personal performance operating system** for users who care about both:
- executing a serious training plan
- improving long-term health and longevity

It should feel like a premium hybrid of:
- coach
- recovery dashboard
- biomarker interpreter
- strategic health advisor
- LLM-based decision layer for both training and longevity

## Core jobs to be done

### Daily job
"Tell me what to do today based on what was planned, what I actually did, and how recovered I am."

### Weekly job
"Show whether my training is on track and where my load/recovery balance is drifting."

### Longitudinal job
"Show what matters most for long-term health based on blood work, trends, and research-backed context."

## Product principles
1. **Action first, data second**
2. **One clear daily recommendation beats five disconnected scores**
3. **Premium calm beats noisy biohacker aesthetics**
4. **Explain the why behind every recommendation**
5. **Health guidance must be prioritized, not encyclopedic**

## Signature product surfaces

### 1. Daily Brief
The hero experience.

Must answer:
- train hard, train moderate, recover, or rest?
- what was planned?
- should today's session be modified?
- what is the key health priority today?

### 2. Plan vs Actual
The core training truth layer.

Must answer:
- what was programmed?
- what was completed?
- where did substitutions happen?
- were deviations smart or problematic?

### 3. Longevity Priorities
The long-horizon layer.

Must answer:
- what biomarker/risk domain matters most now?
- what changed over time?
- what action is recommended?
- when should it be retested?

## MVP boundaries
Do now:
- import-centric architecture
- transparent rules
- single-user experience
- strong dashboard language and IA

Avoid for now:
- giant AI agent claims without underlying data quality
- full medical-grade interpretation engine
- social features
- advanced coach marketplace features
