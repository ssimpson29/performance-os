# Fitness + Longevity App Inspiration

## Patterns to borrow

### Whoop
- strong daily decision framing
- coaching language around readiness and strain
- concise explanation of why today's recommendation changed

### Oura
- premium calm visual tone
- contributor-based insight breakdowns
- soft, trustworthy presentation of health metrics

### Athlytic / Gentler Streak
- Apple ecosystem-native synthesis
- simple train / go easy / recover framing

### Levels / Function / InsideTracker
- biomarker domains instead of raw lab chaos
- trend plus action format

### Fitbod / Future / Trainerize
- plan execution and workout progression feel like guided coaching

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- score overload
- dashboard-as-spreadsheet home screen
- overly clinical blood work presentation
- rigid plans that ignore recovery context
- vague AI insight text detached from actual user state

## Recommended information architecture
- Today
- Plan
- Recovery
- Longevity
- Coach

## Visual direction
- dark premium base
- restrained accent colors
- typography-first hierarchy
- sparklines over huge chart walls
- editorial / concierge tone rather than “hardcore gym” aesthetic
